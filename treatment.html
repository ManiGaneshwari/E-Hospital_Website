Treatment Options at MK Hospitals

Your Health, Our Priority

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive care for heart conditions. Our team of specialists offers treatments for heart disease, hypertension, and other cardiovascular conditions.

  • Heart Surgery
  • Angioplasty
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation

Orthopedics

Our orthopedics department specializes in the treatment of musculoskeletal disorders. We offer surgical and non-surgical treatments for bone, joint, and muscle issues.

  • Knee Replacement
  • Hip Replacement
  • Sports Medicine

Neurology

Our neurology department is dedicated to treating neurological disorders. Our experts provide care for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, and multiple sclerosis.

  • Stroke Treatment
  • Seizure Management
  • Neurological Rehabilitation

Oncology

Our oncology department offers advanced cancer treatments. We provide personalized care for various types of cancer, including chemotherapy, radiation, and surgery.

  • Chemotherapy
  • Radiation Therapy
  • Surgical Oncology
