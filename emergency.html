Emergency Care

Your health is our priority. Available 24/7.

About Our Emergency Care Services

At MK Hospitals, our emergency department is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by highly trained professionals. We provide immediate care for a wide range of emergencies, including trauma, heart attacks, strokes, and more.

Services Offered

  • 24/7 Emergency Response
  • Advanced Trauma Care
  • Cardiac Emergency Services
  • Stroke Care
  • Pediatric Emergency Services
  • On-site Diagnostic Imaging
  • Emergency Surgery

What to Do in an Emergency

If you or someone you know is experiencing a medical emergency, call our emergency hotline immediately at 1800-123-456. Stay calm and follow the instructions provided by our medical professionals.
